Instant drafter's companion for your Cube.

Your CubeCobra ID is the unique identifier in your cube's URL. Navigate to your cube on CubeCobra and look at the address bar — it will look something like cubecobra.com/cube/overview/stone-soup. The part after /overview/ is your ID. It could be a short word, a phrase, or a string of random characters depending on how you set it up.
